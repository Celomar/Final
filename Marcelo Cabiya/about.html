Who are we?

We are a community solely dedicated the pursuit of the most traditional type of thraditional animation there is: FlipBooks.
We have taken it upon ourselves to supply rookies with all the materials and references they need.
As well as all flipbook enthusiasts out there who could be interested in what we have to offer to the world of traditional 2d animation
